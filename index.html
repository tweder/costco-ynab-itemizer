Itemize your Costco receipts into YNAB

A single Costco run is one of the worst things to enter in YNAB: one big transaction you have to split across a dozen categories, with sales tax that applies to some items and not others. By hand it's a slog.

Take a photo of the receipt and this builds the whole entry for you — one YNAB transaction, split line by line, every item categorized, sales tax distributed exactly as the receipt charged it, the real product name in each memo, and the total reconciled to the penny. Review it, fix anything, post.

Runs entirely in your browser. You bring your own Anthropic and YNAB keys — they're sent only to those two services, never to a server.

⚠️ This page is open as a local file, and YNAB rejects posts from a file:// page. Open it from a web address instead: use the hosted version, or serve your own copy — in a terminal, cd into this file's folder, run python3 -m http.server, then open the http://localhost:8000/ address it prints.
